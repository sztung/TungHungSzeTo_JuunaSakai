Biography

Born in Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture, Japan November 2, 2000

JUNNA made her singing career debut as the singing voice of Mikumo Guynemer, the lead vocalist of the group Walküre from the anime Macross Δ. She was only 14 years old when she performed in the unit's digital single "Ikenai Borderline" released at the end of December 2015, making her the youngest debut in the anime franchise with 34 years history
